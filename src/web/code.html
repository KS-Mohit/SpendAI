Privacy by Architecture, Not by Policy

We don't have a privacy policy because we don't have your data. It never leaves your phone.

No Cloud Sync

Traditional apps upload your transactions to their servers. SpendAI processes everything locally on your silicon.

End-to-End Local

From SMS reading to AI categorization, every computation happens within the app's sandbox environment.

Open Source

Don't take our word for it. Inspect the code on GitHub and verify that no network calls are being made.

Secure Storage

Your data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database, protected by your phone's biometric security.
